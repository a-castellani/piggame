Rules


1. Player one starts the game

2. After each roll, the player accumulates points based on the face value of the die:

- If a 1 is rolled, the player's turn ends, and they lose all points gained during that turn.

- If any other number is rolled (2 to 6), the player can choose to continue rolling or stop and add the rolled number to their turn total.

3. Players can continue rolling as long as they want, but if they roll a 1, their turn ends, and they lose all points accumulated in that turn.

4. A player can choose to "hold" at any time during their turn to add their turn total to their overall score and pass the dice to the next player.

5. The game continues with each player taking turns, trying to reach the winning score 100 points without rolling a 1.
